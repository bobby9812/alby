ABOUT ALBY MANGELS
Alby’s acclaimed series of family-rated action-adventure/documentary films, often featuring rare and one-off footage, has achieved extraordinary ratings and revenues world-wide, engaging viewers to watch all episodes. A cross between Indiana Jones and Crocodile Dundee, Alby established his original brand of “reality adventure television” as a model for greats like Bear Grylls, Steve Irwin and Sir David Attenborough. Extreme risks, danger, exotic locations, stunning women, lovable canine companions and incredible talents are the trademark blend that sets Alby apart .. and others try to copy.

Screened on popular networks in several countries including the USA, UK, Australia, Germany, Ireland, Netherlands and New Zealand, Alby Mangels has out-rated block-busters and other popular movies. In the tradition of Sir David Attenborough, Indiana Jones, Jacques Cousteau, Dame Jane Goodall and Anthony Bourdain … Alby is a unique pioneer, ahead of his time.
Alby’s exciting series captures a world before the internet, Corona virus, global warming, rainforest destruction and vanishing wildlife such as the African mountain gorillas.  Trekking around the world exploring over 70 countries and navigating by the stars, this action-adventure series features spectacular remote frontiers, savage war zones, hostile wilderness and obscure cultures.
No other adventurer / film-maker has ever devoted 25 years of his life to such a world-wide accomplishment.
